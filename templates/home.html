📱 How Local-Only Mode Works

Three steps. Under a minute. Maximum privacy.

  1. Tap "Use Local-Only Mode" above — no email or signup needed
  2. Create a passphrase (8+ characters) — this encrypts everything on your device
  3. Start tracking — periods, symptoms, journal. All encrypted. All offline.

Pro tip: On your phone, tap the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen" to install Bloom as an app. It'll work without internet after that.

🔐 Security Details

  • Encryption: AES-256-GCM — the same standard used by banks and governments
  • Key derivation: PBKDF2-SHA256, 210,000 iterations — makes brute-force attacks impractical
  • Auto-lock: Configurable timeout (2/5/10/30 min) locks data if you step away
  • Passphrase change: Re-encrypts all data instantly with your new passphrase
  • Zero-knowledge: Server never receives your data or passphrase. Ever.
  • Backup: Export encrypted or plain JSON to move between devices

☁️ Want cloud sync later?
