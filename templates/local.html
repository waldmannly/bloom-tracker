🔒 Local-Only Tracker

Zero server contact. Your data never leaves this device — encrypted at rest with your passphrase using AES-256-GCM.

Works offline after first visit. Tap your browser menu → "Add to Home Screen" to install as an app.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Create your passphrase below — pick something strong (8+ characters). This encrypts all your data.
  2. Log your cycle — add period dates, symptoms, and journal entries.
  3. Install the app — on your phone, tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen" (Android) or Share → "Add to Home Screen" (iOS).

⚠️ Remember your passphrase! There's no recovery — if you forget it, your data can't be unlocked. Write it down somewhere safe.

🛡️ Device Encryption

All data is encrypted with your passphrase before being saved to this browser. Without the passphrase, the stored data is unreadable — even with physical device access. Your passphrase is never stored or sent anywhere.

💾 Backup & Sync

Export your data to move between devices, keep offline backups, or switch to server mode.

Export Options

  • 🔐 Encrypted Backup — Protected with a passphrase you choose. Safe to store in cloud drives, email to yourself, etc. Only you can open it.
  • 📄 Plain JSON — Unencrypted, human-readable. Use this to import into a Bloom server account or just read your data directly.

Import

Import any Bloom backup file (encrypted or plain). Encrypted files will ask for the passphrase used during export. Importing merges with your existing data.

⚙️ Settings

Configure security and privacy options for your local vault.

⏱️ Auto-Lock Timer

Automatically locks your data after inactivity. If someone picks up your phone, they'll need your passphrase to see anything.

🔑 Change Passphrase

Re-encrypts all your data with a new passphrase. Use this if you think someone saw your passphrase, or just want to update it. Your data stays intact — only the encryption key changes.

☁️ Switch to Cloud Sync

Want cross-device sync, partner features, or email notifications? Move to a server account.

How to switch:

  1. Export your data — in the Backup section above, click "📄 Plain JSON Export"
  2. Create a server account — choose "Cloud sync" during registration
  3. Import your data — go to Settings → Encrypted Backup & Restore → Restore from Backup, upload your JSON file
  4. Erase local data (optional) — once confirmed, use the Danger Zone below to wipe local storage

⚠️ Don't erase local data until you've confirmed everything imported correctly on the server. You can keep both active simultaneously.

⚠️ Danger Zone

Permanently delete all local data from this browser. This cannot be undone. Make sure you've exported a backup first if you want to keep your data.
